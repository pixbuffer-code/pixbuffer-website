Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026

PixBuffer Software Inc. ("PixBuffer", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you actively provide it to us:

  • Contact inquiries — name, email address, and any details you include when you reach out to us directly.
  • AI Readiness Check — this quiz runs entirely in your browser. No answers, scores, or results are transmitted to our servers. No account or email is required.
  • Calendly bookings — when you book a call, Calendly collects your name and email under their own privacy policy.

2. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics scripts. We do not build profiles of visitors or share data with ad networks.

Specifically, we do not use:

  • Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, or any Google tracking pixel
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel or any social media tracking
  • Hotjar, Mixpanel, Segment, or any behavioural analytics tool
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies of any kind

The only browser storage this site uses is functional — for example, remembering your progress through the AI Readiness Check within a single session. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

If we ever introduce analytics or other cookie-based tools in the future, this policy will be updated before deployment and a consent notice will be displayed.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiry or to deliver the service you requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to respond to and resolve your inquiry. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing us.

5. Third-Party Services

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.
